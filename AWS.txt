=>capital Expenditure
=>Operational Expenditure

Cloud can be considered CapEx when large one-time purchases are made for fixed assets, 
like infrastructure for an on-premises data center. On the other hand, 
cloud can be OpEx when costs are tied to day-to-day operations, 
like a monthly cloud-based application subscription.
